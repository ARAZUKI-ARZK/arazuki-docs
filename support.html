ARAZUKI Reorder Copilot — Help & FAQ

Getting started

  1. Install the app from the Shopify App Store
  2. Your product, inventory, and order data will sync automatically
  3. Check the Reorder Priorities table to see which SKUs need attention
  4. Adjust your default Lead Time and Safety Stock in Settings

Frequently Asked Questions

How is the recommended order quantity calculated?

It is based on your average daily sales over the last 30 days, multiplied by (lead time + safety stock days), minus your current inventory.

What do the reorder signals mean?

🔴 Order Now — stock will run out before your next delivery arrives.
🟡 Order Soon — stock is getting low and should be reordered soon.
🟢 In Stock — you have enough stock for now.

Can I change the lead time and safety stock?

Yes. Go to Settings in the app and update your default values.

Is the app free?

The app is currently free to use.
